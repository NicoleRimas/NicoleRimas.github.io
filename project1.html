Big Lagoon

The Big Lagoon is one of the most iconic attractions in El Nido, Palawan. Located on Miniloc Island, it is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty, surrounded by towering limestone cliffs that rise dramatically from the water. The lagoon is accessible by boat or kayak, and its expansive waters are perfect for swimming, kayaking, or simply enjoying the serene atmosphere.

Small Lagoon

The Small Lagoon is a stunning natural gem located on Miniloc Island in El Nido, Palawan. It is characterized by its crystal-clear turquoise waters, surrounded by dramatic limestone cliffs that create a secluded, peaceful atmosphere. The lagoon is accessible by kayak or small boat through a narrow entrance, which adds an adventurous element to the visit.

Secret Lagoon

The Secret Lagoon is one of El Nido's most enchanting and hidden gems, located on Miniloc Island in Palawan. As its name suggests, the lagoon is somewhat of a secret, tucked away behind a narrow opening in a limestone cave. Visitors must swim or kayak through this small entrance to reach the lagoon, which adds an element of adventure and mystery to the experience.

Snake Island

Snake Island, also known as "Vigan Island," is a unique and picturesque sandbar located in El Nido, Palawan. It is renowned for its distinctive snake-like shape, which emerges from the turquoise waters at low tide. The island offers panoramic views of the surrounding islands and pristine waters, making it a popular spot for swimming, sunbathing, and exploring.
